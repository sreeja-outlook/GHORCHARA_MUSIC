About GHORCHARA

Born in the heart of Kolkata's vibrant music scene, GHORCHARA emerged as a force that defies conventional boundaries. Our name, meaning 'wanderers' in Bengali, reflects our journey of musical exploration and spiritual freedom. We are more than just a band - we're storytellers of urban tales, weaving together the raw energy of rock with the soul-stirring essence of Bengali culture.

Drawing inspiration from the streets of Kolkata to the cosmic soundscapes of Mohiner Ghoraguli, Fossils, Cactus, Warfaze, and many more, our music is a bold fusion of progressive rock and Bengali folk elements. Each performance is crafted to take our audience on a transcendent journey, breaking free from the ordinary and embracing the extraordinary.

With Rahul's electrifying guitar work and ear-soothing vocals, Mainak's innovative rhythms and Shivam's masterful guitar work and sound engineering, we create a sound that's uniquely our own. Behind the scenes, Sreeja manages our digital presence and visual documentation while developing her musical skills to join the band soon. Our lyrics speak of rebellion, love, social consciousness, and the human spirit - resonating with those who dare to dream differently and challenge the status quo.
